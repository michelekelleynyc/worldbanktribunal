Jury's Findings
  
Alejandro Nadal
 


"I come from a region of the world where there is hope. There are winds of change that are taking place in the present in Latin America. There are many, many dimensions and levels or our lives and it's not only in country like Venezuela, which everybody will think immediately, but of Equador, to a lesser degree Bolivia and even little bit of Argentina. But I'm talking of every level of life : social life, political life in all countries of Latin America.

See the Jury's Biographies
  
So I am very optimistic that people are mobilizing. People are becoming aware and people are exactly what we are tonight in this auditorium with the firm conviction that there are many more good things happening than evil or bad things and I think, if you think about it, you immediately will agree withe me."
