Your support is urgently needed.

Now that the Tribunal is over, there are important expenses that must be covered moving forward in order to ensure the lessons learned at the Tribunal are not lost.

During the Tribunal, many people asked "who" was funding this event. While a few donors have provided needed assistance to fill gaps, the event was largely sponsored by the convening organizations themselves, and was put together on a minimal budget.

Your donation to the Independent People's Tribunal on The World Bank in India will provide crucial assistance to basic, but essential tasks such as circulating the materials gathered at the Event. Once these basic costs are covered, it is hoped that funds will also be raised to formally document the event in print and film. Please help the Tribunal meet these goals!
