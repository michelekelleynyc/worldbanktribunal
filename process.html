Process
The Tribunal on the WBG and its allies is visualized as a collective process that was guided by key organizations and groups from across the country. The Tribunal was thus structured:

  1. Panel (jury)– Heard the Depositions and render judgement. Made up of distinguished individuals from within and outside of India jointly selected by the convenors, advisors and secretariat.
  2. Convenors–Played the most critical role in the process. They were key groups/ grassroot organizations and movements who were responsible for a sector or region. They assisted the participant groups in mobilizing affected people, putting together the Depositions and in selecting the projects to be profiled.
  3. Advisors– Individuals with expertise in the field who were guided, help with the research and assist the process.
  4. Endorsing/Participating Organisations– Large spectrum of groups/grassroot organisations who endorsed and supported the process.
  5. Secretariat– Comprising of researchers and organizers who provided administrative and logistic support to the process and assisted with research.
