Radio & TV Coverage
The Independant People's Tribunal on the World Bank Group in India was announced by Delhi radio station, Radio One, on the 19th, 20th and 21st September 2007. Hear the radio announcement:
On 24 September, CNBC TV - 18 covered the event. The CNBC Television piece focuses on the so called "Revolving Door", e.g. the conflict of interest between many Indian bureacrats who accept consultancies, travel grants and other honorarium from the World Bank.
