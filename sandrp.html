South Asian Network for Dams, Rivers,
and People (SANDRP)
www.sandrp.in
SANDRP is an informal network of organization and individuals working on issues related to water and sector. SANDRP areas of interest include all issues related to water sector, with special focus on issues associated with large dams, mostly in India, but including south Asia regional issues. SANDRP’s
Water
  
Broad objective is to work on issues so that democracy, people and environment get due place. Started in 1998, SANDRP activities monitoring research, documentation , policy analysis, publication, dissemination, advocacy, networking and awareness building among organizations and people related to the issues. Publishes a journal “Dams, Rivers & People
