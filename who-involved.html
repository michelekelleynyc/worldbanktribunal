PARTICIPANTS
Key groups, individuals and organisations are playing the roles of convenors and advisors. The process has attempted to be inclusive as possible.

Jury

The jury / panel sat before the tribunal, heard depositions and passed a final judgement. The jury members were selected by the groups involved in the tribunal. the juryist are well known experts from their field.

Convenors

The convenors were the most important aspect of the people;s movement. They are key groups, grassroot organizations and movements who are responsible for a sector or region. They played a vital role in mobilizing people, putting together the depositions and organizing the event.

Endorsers

Over 50 local, national and international groups have endorsed the Tribunal and are involved at various levels in the Tribunal.
